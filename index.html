某些人生哲学

Perhaps you knew something

What we do for ourselves dies with us. What we do for others that 's beyond us.

The all exchanges of us, the petty irritations, deadly revelations, flat announcements of disaster.

The grunts and poetry of life--Everything we've ever said. I heard a melody--All our destinies flying and swimming in blood and emptiness.
